Protect Legendary Animal Legendary animal

Co-op: free a Legendary Animal from poachers and escort it to safety. Do not get too close. After the event you can often study or sample it in free roam. Twice per day.

Wild Animal Tagging Possible legendary animal

Sedate and tag specific species within ~10 minutes. A bonus Legendary Animal may appear, but it is not guaranteed. Twice per day.

On the map sidebar you can configure sound alerts: in advance (for a chosen number of minutes) and when each event starts.
